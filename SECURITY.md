# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of this project seriously. If you believe you have found a security vulnerability, please do not report it through a public issue or pull request. Instead, please follow the steps below:

1.  **Private Report:** Email the details of the vulnerability to [security@example.com](mailto:security@example.com).
2.  **Details to Include:**
    - A clear description of the vulnerability.
    - Steps to reproduce (a proof-of-concept is highly appreciated).
    - Potential impact.
3.  **Response Time:** We aim to acknowledge receipt of your report within 48 hours and provide a timeline for resolution within 5 business days.

## Out of Scope

- Theoretical vulnerabilities without a working proof-of-concept.
- Social engineering or physical attacks.
- Denial of Service (DoS) attacks.

## Safe Harbor

We will not take legal action against individuals who discover and report vulnerabilities in accordance with this policy, provided they act in good faith and do not exploit the vulnerability for malicious purposes or disclose it publicly before a fix is released.

Thank you for helping us keep this project secure!
